---

## Stakeholder Communication Framework

**Situation → Complication → Resolution → Recommendation**

Example: "We need microservices."
- **S:** E-commerce plateau at 2K RPS
- **C:** Monolith deploy risk, 4-hour releases
- **R:** Strangler 2 bounded contexts Q1–Q2
- **Recommendation:** Don't split all 40 modules — start with checkout

---

## Common Behavioral Questions

1. Tell me about a time you disagreed with a tech decision.
2. How do you prioritize technical debt vs features?
3. Describe explaining a complex outage to non-technical leadership.
4. How do you handle a team that won't adopt your architecture standards?
5. When did you change your mind based on team input?

## Practice Exercise

Write 3 STAR stories (500 words each):
- **S**ituation — context, stakes
- **T**ask — your responsibility
- **A**ction — what YOU did (not "we")
- **R**esult — metrics, learnings
