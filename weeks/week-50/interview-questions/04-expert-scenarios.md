## Q101: Staff Panel Stakeholder

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| **Difficulty** | Expert |
| **Category** | Leadership |
| **Frequency** | Very Common |
| **Type** | Scenario |

### Question

Staff panel: prove you managed a hostile stakeholder without burning bridge.

### Short Answer (30 seconds)

STAR with empathy, data, escalation path, relationship outcome, lesson learned — show patience and firmness.

### Detailed Answer

**Strong answer elements:**
- Hostile behavior described factually not emotionally
- Your listening and criteria approach
- Escalation with options not tattle
- Relationship today: professional, productive
- Lesson: pre-wire and document

**Probe ready:** 'What would you do differently?'

### Architecture Perspective

Staff panel behavioral depth exceeds textbook fundamentals.

### Follow-up Questions

1. **Failed relationship? — Honest if repaired — or lesson from failure.**
2. **HR involvement? — Mention if appropriate boundary crossed.**

### Common Mistakes in Interviews

- Villainize stakeholder
- Claim never had conflict
- No measurable outcome
