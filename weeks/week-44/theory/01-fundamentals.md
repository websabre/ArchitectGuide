## Review Framework (Apply to Each)

### 1. Requirements (15 min)
- Functional vs non-functional
- Constraints (compliance, budget, timeline)
- Unknowns to clarify with stakeholders

### 2. High-Level Design (30 min)
- C4 Context + Container diagrams
- Data flow, integration points
- Technology choices with ADR rationale

### 3. Deep Dive (30 min)
- Scale, security, failure modes
- Cost estimate (order of magnitude)
- Migration / rollout plan

### 4. Presentation (15 min)
- Executive summary (2 min)
- Technical detail (10 min)
- Risks and mitigations (3 min)
