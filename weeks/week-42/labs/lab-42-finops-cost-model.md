# Lab 42: FinOps Cost Model

| **Week** | 42 | **Duration** | See steps |

## Objectives

Build cost model spreadsheet for Azure workload.

## Steps

1. List all resources with SKUs
2. Estimate monthly cost at 10K and 100K users
3. Identify top 3 cost drivers
4. Propose 3 optimizations with savings %
5. Present to mock CFO in 5 min
