## STAR Template

```markdown
### Story: [Title]
**Category:** Technical leadership
**Prompt:** Tell me about a time you led a major technical change.

**Situation:** (2-3 sentences)
**Task:** (1 sentence — your role)
**Action:** (4-6 bullets — specific steps YOU took)
**Result:** (metrics: %, $, time; plus learning)
```

---

## Daily Drill

| Day | Activity |
|-----|----------|
| Mon–Wed | Write 2 stories/day (6 total) |
| Thu | Record yourself — 3 min per story |
| Fri | Mock behavioral interview with partner |
| Sat–Sun | Refine weak stories; trim to 3 min each |

**Anti-pattern:** Generic "we" stories without metrics or personal actions.
