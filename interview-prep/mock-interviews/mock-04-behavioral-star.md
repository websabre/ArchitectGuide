# Mock Interview 04 — Behavioral (STAR)

> **Duration:** 30 minutes | **Weeks 50–51 prep

## Format

4 questions × 7 minutes each. Interviewer probes Action and Result.

## Questions

1. **Technical leadership:** "Tell me about a time you drove a major architecture decision."
2. **Conflict:** "Describe disagreeing with a tech lead or PM on a technical approach."
3. **Failure:** "Tell me about an architecture decision that didn't work. What did you learn?"
4. **Executive communication:** "Explain a complex technical outage to non-technical leadership."

## Interviewer Probe Bank

After each story:
- "What was YOUR specific role vs the team?"
- "What metrics improved?"
- "What would you do differently?"
- "How did stakeholders react?"

## Red Flags

- "We did..." without individual contribution
- No metrics in Result
- Blaming others
- No learning in failure story

## Scoring

| Dimension | Weight |
|-----------|--------|
| STAR structure | 25% |
| Specific actions | 25% |
| Measurable results | 25% |
| Self-awareness | 25% |
